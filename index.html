Office of the Citizen

An independent civic institution
for public record, policy,
and accountability.

Office of the Citizen is an independent non-governmental organisation building civic records, research, and policy work for Nigeria. Through its projects, it documents institutional conduct, studies recurring public failures, and develops serious responses grounded in method, evidence, and public consequence.
