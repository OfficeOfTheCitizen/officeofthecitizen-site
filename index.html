Public Record

A public record of how power is exercised.

Office of the Citizen builds structured, evidence-based records of governance conduct. It is designed to document institutional failure, accountability responses, and public consequence with methodological discipline.

Principles

Evidence before opinion

Records are assembled from verifiable sources, documentary trails, and structured factual claims before interpretation.

Institutions, not personalities

The focus is governance conduct, administrative systems, and public decision-making, not spectacle or personal drama.

Public record, built to last

The archive is designed for long-term civic use: reference, scrutiny, retrieval, and institutional memory.

Institution

Designed as an enduring civic archive for Nigeria, and built to scale.

Office of the Citizen is being developed as a durable public record system for accountability work that requires method, continuity, and trust.
