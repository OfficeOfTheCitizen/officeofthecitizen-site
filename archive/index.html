The Archive

A public record of how power is exercised.

The Archive is Office of the Citizen’s permanent civic record of public consequence in Nigeria’s Fourth Republic. It documents how power is exercised, identifies responsibility across the accountability chain, preserves evidence trails, and makes institutional conduct available for public understanding, scrutiny, and long-term analysis. It is not a scandal vault, not a news site, and not a political tool. It is a structured civic record built for permanence.
